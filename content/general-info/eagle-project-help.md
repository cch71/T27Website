### Eagle Scout Rank Application

You do not need to complete the rank application yourself. Instead, obtain Eagle
Application Assistance form from Advancement Chair when all requirements are
complete, including Scoutmaster conference. Once you receive this form you must
obtain all appropriate signatures and then deliver to the Council office for validation.
