### Prohibited Items for boys at any scouting activity
::list{type="danger"}
- Cellphones
- Radio’s, TV’s or electronic games
- Aerosol products
- Lighters or tobacco products
- Alcohol or illegal substances
- Firearms or bows and arrows
- Sling shots
- Fixed blade or sheath knives
